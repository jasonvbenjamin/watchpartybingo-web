Privacy Policy

Last updated June 19, 2026

Watch Party Bingo lets you play live bingo with friends around the shows, games, and events you watch together. This policy explains what information the app and web player collect, how it's used, and the choices you have. We aim to collect as little as possible.

Information we collect

  • Anonymous account. When you start or join a game, we create an anonymous account (a random identifier) so your game can sync across devices. You don't need to give an email or password to play.
  • Display name. The name you enter for a game, shown to the other players in that same game.
  • Gameplay data. The games you host or join, the squares you mark, your score, and win claims — so the live board and leaderboard work.
  • Photos ("Snaps"). If you choose to capture or upload a photo during a game, we store it so it can be shown to the players in that game. Snaps are kept private to that game's members.
  • Beta sign-ups. If you ask to join the beta, we store the email address you provide so we can contact you about the app.
  • Purchases. Premium purchases are processed by Apple. We receive confirmation of what you're entitled to — never your card or payment details.
  • Basic technical data. Like most online services, our infrastructure provider processes standard request data (such as IP address and timestamps) to operate and secure the service.

How we use it

  • Run your games and sync state live between players.
  • Show names, scores, leaderboards, and Snaps to the players in your game.
  • Provide premium features you've purchased.
  • Contact you if you signed up for the beta.
  • Keep the service working, secure, and free of abuse.

How it's shared

We don't sell your personal information, and we don't use it for third-party advertising. Data is processed by the providers that run the app for us — our backend host (Supabase) and Apple (for purchases and push). We may disclose information if required by law or to protect the safety and integrity of the service.

Data retention & your choices

  • Game data is kept so you and other players have game history; we remove it when it's no longer needed to run the service.
  • You can ask us to access or delete your data, including Snaps you've shared, by emailing the address below.
  • You can leave a game at any time, and you can decline to share photos.

Children

Watch Party Bingo isn't directed to children under 13 (or under 16 in the EEA/UK). We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us information, contact us and we'll delete it.

Changes

We may update this policy as the app evolves. We'll revise the "last updated" date above, and significant changes will be reflected here.
